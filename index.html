Overview

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) emerging on Ethereum is showing promising result towards during a disintermediated financial system. But most protocols exist today lack a systematic way for managing risk and handling system insolvency. In this paper, we will discuss what is the common pitfall for solvency management in DeFi nowdays, and proposed that some constraints can be relieved. We will use balance sheet as tool, and explain how coverage ratio and quick ratio can be used for risk assessment of the system. Lastly, we will combine all these and propose a protocol that can unify all DeFi protocols (including but not limited to: token swapping, borrowing, portfolio management) into one system and thereby significantly increase capital efficiency.

For this dissertation, we would like to construct Decentral Bank - an autonomous financial protocol constructed on public, decentralized blockchain (i.e.: Ethereum for now).
